My JeevanSathi
"My JeevanSathi" is your PERSONAL SECTION which you get Free on becoming a Member. Just Log-in (with your username & password) to access the Features, which include:
1. Profile Statistics  
  i. No. of times your Profile has been viewed;
  ii. Your Profile Position;
  iii. Check Members who have contacted you;
  iv. Check Members you have contacted;
  v. Membership Status.
2. Profile Management
  i. Edit Your Profile;
  ii. Input/Edit Partner's Profile;
  iii. Manage Your Photo(s);
  iv. Bookmark Profiles.
3. Contact Members
  i. Search, Browse & Contact Profiles.
