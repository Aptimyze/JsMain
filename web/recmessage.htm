Members who contact you
1. Free Members can Receive "Full Contact" from Full Members and "Initial Contact" from other Free Members.  
2. Full Members can Receive "Full Contact" from Full Members and "Initial Contact" from Free Members. They can however make "Full Contact" with the Free Members who have sent them an "Initial Contact".
"Full Contact" means sending a personal & customised message alongwith contact details to Members directly to their email id. The recipient can thus respond to sender's email id directly.
"Initial Contact" means sending a standard message to members expressing interest in their profile(s). The recipient can then view the senders' profile and can accept or decline the contact. On becoming a Full Member, the sender or the recipient can contact each other directly (ie. make "Full Contact").
