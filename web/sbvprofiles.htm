Search, View & Contact Profiles
To contact Members, you are required to Log-in to "My JeevanSathi" and use the facility of "Advanced Search", "Quick Search" or "View Profiles" or "Photo Club".
1. "Advanced Search" will enable you to shortlist matches as per detailed parameters - on the basis of 20 criteria. You can then view Individual Member Profiles and contact them.  
2. "Quick Search" will enable you to sort matches as per basic (ie. broad) parameters. You can then view Individual Member Profiles and contact them.
3. "View Profile" will enable you to view an Individual Member Profile by entering the case-sensitive username. You can then contact the member.
4. "Photo Club" will enable you to sort all members which have a photogaph, by gender. You can then view Individual Member Profiles and contact them.
It may be noted that all "Advanced Search", "Quick Search" and "Photo Club" Results appear in a reverse chronological order. This means that the latest input/edited profiles appear first and then the older profiles.
The Profiles keep getting pushed down as new profiles are added. However, ALL Members can Push their Profile to the TOP, whenever they Edit or Update it.
