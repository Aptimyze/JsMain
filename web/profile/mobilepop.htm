Mobile Payment Gateway
You can now make a payment using your mobile phone using this service. All you need to do is register with your bank (presently only for Citibank) for this service.
Your bank will issue you a pin which can be then used to authenticate your transaction to ensure a safe and secure payment mechanism.
Your mobile number can be mapped to either your credit card or debit card.
Currently supported only by Citibank. Your mobile number will not be used for any marketing/promotional activities to ensure privacy.
