Be proactive in making contacts
After Log-in to "My JeevanSathi":
1 Access the Individual Profile Page of a Member.
2 Scroll Down & you will find a clickable button of either "Initiate Contact" or "Full Contact".
3 Free Members can make "Initial Contact" with ALL members.
"Initial Contact" means sending a standard message to members expressing interest in their profile. The recipient can then view the sender's profile & can accept or decline the contact. On becoming a e-Rishta Member, the sender or the recipient can contact each other directly (ie. establish "Full Contact").
4 e-Rishta Members can make "Full Contact" with ALL members.
"Full Contact" means sending a personal & customised message alongwith contact details to members directly to their e-mail id. The recipient can thus respond to the sender's e-mail id directly.
