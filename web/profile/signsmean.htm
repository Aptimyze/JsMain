What do these signs mean?
Contact Accepted: The initial contact with this member has been accepted.
Contact Declined: The initial contact with this member has been declined.
Contact Open: The initial contact with this member is still open (neither accepted nor declined).
Contact Cancelled: The contact with this person has been cancelled.
Bookmarked Profile: This profile is in your Bookmarks.
Photo Profile: The profile has a photograph attached to it.
eValuePack: The profile has its contact details visible.
eRishta: This profile has the privilege to send personalised messages.
Online Member: The user is online and you can chat with him using the Jeevansathi Messenger.
Horoscope: Horoscope lagan chart available.
Marriage Bureau: Profile posted by Marriage Bureau.
SMS Contact: Initiate contact through sms available.
