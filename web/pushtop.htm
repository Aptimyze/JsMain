Profile Pushed to the Top
In "Advanced Search", "Quick Search" and "Photo Club" results, all profiles appear in a reverse Chronological order. This means that the latest profiles (input/edited) appear first and then the older profiles.
The Profiles keep getting pushed down as new profiles are added. However, ALL Members can Push their Profile to the TOP, whenever they Edit or Update it.
To check out your "Profile Position" Log-in to "My JeevanSathi".
