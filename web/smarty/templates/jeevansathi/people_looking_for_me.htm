Members Interested In Being My Jeevansathi

This feature lists members whose Preferred Partner requirements you fulfill.

For example:
  • Sonali is a 23 year old tall, fair and convent educated Jain girl, working as a school teacher. She is looking for a Jain businessman in the age group of 25-28, between 5'8" and 6'2".
  • Mukesh is a 28 year old, 5'9" tall Jain businessman.
As a result:
  • Mukesh matches Sonali’s partner requirements. Sonali’s name will be included in his Members interested in being my Jeevansathi list.
  • However, Sonali may not have what Mukesh is looking for. Her profile may not match Mukesh’s partner requirements so his name will not be included in her Members interested in being my Jeevansathi list. For a perfect match, you will need to use Two Way match.
