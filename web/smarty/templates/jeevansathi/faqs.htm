Q 1. Can I input my Profile Free on JeevanSathi.Com?
    Yes, you can input your Profile Free on JeevanSathi.Com. Thus, you will become a Free Member which will enable you to receive response from lacs of other members; and also make contacts with other members, as follows:  
    i. Send INITIAL CONTACT to ALL members;  
    ii. Receive INITIAL CONTACTS from FREE Members;  
    iii. Receive FULL CONTACTS from FULL Members.  
    "Full Contact" means sending a personal & customised message alongwith contact details to Members directly to their email id. The recipient can thus respond to sender's email id directly.  
  "Initial Contact" means sending a standard message to members expressing interest in their profile(s). The recipient can then view the senders' profile and can accept or decline the contact. On becoming a Full Member, the sender or the recipient can contact each other directly (ie. make "Full Contact").
Q 2. Can I Search and Contact Free on JeevanSathi.Com?
  Yes, you can Search and Contact members Free.
  You can use the following Search Options:
    i. Advanced Search will enable you to shortlist matches as per detailed parameters - on the basis of 20 criteria.  
    ii. Quick Search will enable you to sort matches as per basic (ie. broad) parameters  
    iii. Photo Club will enable you to genderwise view all members with photos.  
    iv. View profile will enable you to view an Individual Member Profile by entering the case-sensitive username.  
    It is necessary to Register as a Member (ie. Input Your Profile which is Free) to contact other Members. This enables your information/details to be auto-transmitted to the recipient.  
    You can contact ALL members and receive response from ALL members - for details refer Q1 hereinabove.  
Q 3. What are the other Free Member Privileges?
  After Registration (Input of Profile), a Free Member can use the Search Options, Make Contacts & Receive Response from other members. Other Free Member privileges include:
    i. Get Your Personal Section - Your Profile Manager "My JeevanSathi".  
    ii. Input Partner's Profile - Get Free Daily Recommendations and more ...  
    iii. Add Multiple Photos - Create Your Own Photo Album.  
    iv. Push Your Profile to the Top!  
Q 4. What is a Full Member?
  As a Full Member you will enjoy numerous privileges as hereunder:
  i. Make FULL CONTACT with ALL members (Free or Paid);
  ii. Also make Full Contact with ALL Responses received to your profile;
  iii. Get the "Message Retrieval Service" - you can store a personal message which is retrieved while making Full Contact.
  iv. View "Contact Information" like address and telephone numbers of ALL those profiles who have entered these. It may however be noted that only some members choose to mention these in their profiles.
  "Full Contact" means sending a personal & customised message alongwith contact details to Members directly to their email id. The recipient can thus respond to sender's email id directly.
Q 5. What is a Value Added Member?
  As a Value Added Member you will enjoy numerous privileges as hereunder:
  i. ALL "Full Member" benefits (mentioned in Q. 4 hereinabove);
  ii. Your Profile will appear in a coloured band and the username in bold, in Search Results.
Q 6. What is "Full Contact"?
  "Full Contact" means sending a personal & customised message alongwith contact details to Members directly to their email id. The recipient can thus respond to sender's email id directly.
Q 7. What is an "Initial Contact"?
  Free Members can contact ALL members and can receive response from FREE members through "Initial Contact".
  When you contact by making an "Initial Contact":
  i. A standard message is sent directly to the recipients e-mail id that a JeevanSathi Member has initiated contact;
  ii. The recipient will be able to view the profile of the sender by a) clicking on the link mentioned in the "Initial Contact" or b) after logging in to his account "My JeevanSathi" wherein the details are displayed;
  iii. The contact details (like e-mail id) of the sender are not sent - hence the recipient cannot directly contact the sender;
  iv. The recipient can log-in to his account (My JeevanSathi) and can then view the profile of the sender and can "Accept" or "Decline" the contact;
  v. The recipient's response of "Accept" or "Decline" is then transmitted to the sender (to the sender's e-mail id);
  vi. Either the sender or the recipient has to then become a Full Member to access the contact details and will then be able to CONTACT DIRECTLY and send complete message.
Q 8. How do I become a Full or Value Added Member?
 

Click on Full Member or Value Added Member. Membership options are available for 3 months, 6 months and 1 Year. Payment can be made by any of the following options:

    iii. By Draft or cheque.
