1. Open the URL www.jeevansathi.com

2. Login with your user name and password.

3. When you have seen a member’s profile carefully, just click on the “Express Interest” link on profile. Now you have made an initial contact with a jeevansathi.com member. An initial contact can be made by both FREE and PAID MEMBERS. You can initiate chat and send personalized messages along with your contact details only when you are a paid member.
