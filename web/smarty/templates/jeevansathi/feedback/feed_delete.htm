Instructions on how to delete your profile

1. Open the URL www.jeevansathi.com

2. Login with your user name and password.

3. Click on ‘Settings’ on the top of the page

4. Select ‘Hide / Delete link’ on the left pannel

5. You can temporarily hide your profile for 7, 15, 30 days by specifying your password, also you can delete your profile by giving reason and your password.

6. If you delete your profile you will not be able to register again with this email id however you can send a request at webmaster@jeevansathi.com to reactivate your profile.
