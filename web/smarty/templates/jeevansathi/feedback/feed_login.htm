Steps for clearing cache and cookies

Please enter your username and password correctly by taking care of lower, upper case and special characters like -, ., @, #, _ etc. If you still face any issue then Please follow the instructions given below to clear cache and cookies.

Change Browser Settings in IE 6.0 as per the instructions given below:

1. Click on “Tools”.

2. Click on “Internet Options”

3. Click on “Delete Cookies”

 

4. Click on “Ok”

5. Click on “Delete Files”

6. Check the box “Delete on all offline contents”.

7. Click on “Ok”

8. Click on “Advanced”

9. Click on “Restore Defaults”

10. Click on “Apply”

11. Click on “Ok”
