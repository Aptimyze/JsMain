WHAT IS PHOTO BROWSE?

It is our new and exclusive value-added feature that makes your matrimonial search easier, precise, time-saving and interesting too! Now you can search Photo Profiles by shortlisting profiles based on the following criteria:

  • AGE: Enter the minimum and the maximum age limit of the kind of partner you are looking for. This way you can filter out unwanted profiles and search only the profiles of the members in the age group you are looking for.


  • CASTE: Go through profiles of people whose caste matches yours. If caste is not such an important criteria for you, you can skip this section by clicking the "Go" button.


  • MOTHER TONGUE: Choose the profiles of members who match your mother tongue or particular region or area that you are looking for.:For e.g: Punjabi, Bengali etc.


  • COUNTRY: Make your search more specific by looking into the profiles of residents from only that particular country where you want to settle down. For e.g: A long time resident of U.K. would prefer a partner from the same country and now with photo browse, it’s more convenient to find your dream match without searching the entire globe!.
