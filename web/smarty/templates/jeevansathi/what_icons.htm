What do these icons mean?
Close [x]

  • Erishta

    This member is an eRishta customer. This service enables them to view contact details of the accepted members.

  • Evalue

    This member is an eValue customer. In addition to the benefits of eRishta membership, this service lets them show their contact details to everybody.

  • Match Point

    This member is a match point customer. Their profile was created at one of Jeevansathi's match points and is handled by a Jeevansathi operator.

  • Verified Phone Number

    The phone number in this profile exists and has been verified by the member.

  • Invalid phone number

    The phone number in this profile was identified as being invalid.

  • Shortlisted Member

    This member is in your shortlist. You can view the shortlist by clicking on the 'Shortlisted Members' link in Contact Center.

  • Ignored Member

    This member is in your ignored list. Members you ignore cannot contact you. You can view and edit the list of members you ignored by clicking on the 'Ignored Members' link in Contact Center.

  • Horoscope available

    This member's horoscope is available. You can view the horoscope by clicking on this icon in their profile. You can also generate your own horoscope and avail of our 'Astro Compatibility' Service.

  • Recommended Profile

    This profile is recommended to you by Jeevansathi based on your preferences and the profile's preferences. The chance you getting accepted by this profile is high.

  • Highly Recommended Profile

    This profile is highly recommended to you by Jeevansathi based on your preferences and the profile’s preferences. The chance you getting accepted by this profile is very high.

  • Profile previously viewed

    This member’s profile was viewed by you previously.

  • New Profile

    There is new incoming activity from this profile. If you find this icon on a profile, it indicates that the profile has been created since your last visit to the site.

  • HIV +ve

    This member has declared himself/herself to be a HIV positive.
