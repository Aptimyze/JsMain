What is the Business Sathi Programme?
Jeevansathi.com, one of India's leading matrimonial sites, is looking for business partners. To earn additional revenue, all you have to do is put our banner on your website. We will give you a percentage of our profits for every Paid Registration and also a commission on every FREE Registration that we get through your website. For your information, we offer the industry's best pay-outs.

Do I get paid even for Free registrations?
Yes and that's where we differ from the other internet affiliates programme. We will pay a commission of Rs. 10 for each FREE Registration through your site. Provided the user after registering on JeevanSathi.com also 'Actives' his account.

What does 'Activate' mean?
After filling out the Registration Form on Jeevansathi.com, a mail will be sent to the user where he will have to activate his account on Jeevansahti.com by clicking on an Activate button.
