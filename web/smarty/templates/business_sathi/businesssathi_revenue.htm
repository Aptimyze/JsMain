 Registration Type  Commission (Per registration)

For each Free Registration we will pay you a commission of Rs. 10. Provided the user after registering on JeevanSathi.com also ‘Activates his account. After filling out the Registration Form, a mail will be sent to the user where he will have to activate his account on Jeevansathi.com by clicking on an Activate button.
In case of Paid Registrations, a commission of 25%, 30%, and 40% of the membership fee will be paid if the number of paid referrals through your site are 1 to 50, 51-200 and above 201 respectively.
If a user through your site does not gets registered on his first visit to our site but becomes a Free member even by directly visiting our site within 1 week of his first visit, you will still be credited for the Referral Fee accruing from that registration and activation thereafter. If the same member upgrades to a Paid membership within 30 days of his Free Registration, then too, you will be entitled to a commission accruing from that Paid Registration.
