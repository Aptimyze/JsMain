History of the brand
Peter England was launched in the year 1889, in a small building in Strand Road, Londonderry, England. The times were fraught with wartime tension and conflict. This was the gritty context against which the brand we know today as Peter England was quietly forged.

The company, in its support of the ongoing war effort, supplied khaki-colored uniforms for the British troops. Over the years the company went from strength to strength, building up to a peak production of 0.7 million shirts a year, during the 1935-45 war.

In 1955, the product range was extended to include leisure shirts and dress shirts. Business was now growing by leaps and bounds. In 1961, the factory was declared the most up to date in the whole of Europe. In 1968, the company got merged with Viyella federation. Soon, in public consciousness, Peter England made the obvious and well-deserved transition from ceremonial military accouterment to fashionable menswear label.

In India, the success story of Peter England started with the creation of the first international brand to be launched in the country's mid-priced shirt segment and sold 400,000 shirts in the first year. Peter England continues to the best selling shirt in India. At the same time consumer also like wearing Peter England Trousers, Suits & a whole range of Casuals to fit every need & occasion of the Indian Male.

Brand philosophy / mission
For starters, what makes Peter England so special is the way it's crafted. It has 2 piece yoke to ensure proper fit. It has reinforced pockets for extra durability. The fabric is completely fade- resistant and shrink-proof. This is the reaffirmation of Peter England's commitment to quality because Peter England believes style and product quality are the keystones of its success.

To get to the defining essence of the brand, Peter England has always aimed at marrying world-class quality with contemporary fashion norms. The brand has, over the years, consistently come up with product innovations across its entire range while simultaneously ensuring that the brand and its offerings stay as stylishly contemporary as they are affordable.

Product portfolio
The brand addresses practically every corner of a man's wardrobe. The range spans regular work-wear, special occasion business wear, evening formals, casual wear and accessories. Every product category contains within itself a spectrum of styles and price points.

Power brand
Peter England has been rated as one of the top 50 brands across product categories, earning the distinction of being the youngest and only readymade garment brand to appear in this list.

Peter England has a full-fledged design studio in Bangalore. The studio constantly works on product and design development, helping Peter England incorporate the latest international trends in fashion and clothing styles into its brand portfolio. The brand continues to increasing its value addition to every aspect of the product right from its styling to making use of new technologies and has even changed it's packaging as a part of the new strategy. All these initiatives are perpetually backed by a philosophy that ensures consumers gets Affordable Fashion.
