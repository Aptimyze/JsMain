About Sia Solemates

Sia Solemates is a brand extension of Sia Art Jewellery, offering designer bags and footwear. The parent company of the brand is Sia Lifestyles.

Sia Solemates started as an offshoot of Sia Art Jewellery, extending the jewellery franchise into the bags and footwear space, retailing traditional ethnic bridal footwear and bags. The brand met with an enthusiastic response, and today, Sia Solemates houses more than 500 designs in various designs, ranging from casual flats, to occasion-based kitten heels and strappy stilletoes for evening and formal wear as well.

Sia Solemates has more than 300 designs of bags- ranging from embroidered clutches to jute totes to jeweled mobile pouches to sequined carry-alls.

Sia Solemates retails from 5 properties across 3 cities in India and has ambitious expansion plans.
