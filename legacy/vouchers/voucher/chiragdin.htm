About Charagh Din

The genesis of this mega shirt store started with a humble beginning in 1947. As a small tailoring boutique, Charagh Din has ascended to become the favourite of many of the rich and famous, the bold and the beautiful. Presently, Charagh Din has applied for a listing in the Guinness Book of World Records as ‘the largest shirt store on Planet Earth’. The Limca Book of Records already acknowledges the CD store as the largest shirt store in the world.

Charagh Din offers an unimaginable range of shirts at any point of time. A dedicated team of five designers create more than 100 new shirt styles, everyday. Of these, just 20-25 creations make the grade for retailing every day. Just to make sure that every shirt that’s created definitely rocks.

Sink into the largest range of styles, fabrics, cuts, collars and designs -- starting from Rs 330 onwards.

To make the store truly complete, Charagh Din also offers a variety of Casualwear, Business Shirts, Sports Shirts, Designer Shirts, Golfwear and T-shirts.

Add to this, over 200 different Checks and 200 different stripes, wide collars, polo neck Tees, stripes, V-necks, Boat necks, and imported & Indian linens. And there’s ditto – a hip, unisex brand for teenagers.


If the store didn’t seem large enough, Charagh Din also houses its entire collection on one comprehensive website – www.charaghdin.com. One click here or at www.cdshirts.com and you can enter a universe of vibrant, electric party shirts. You can chat here, live, during working hours. Or order shirts that are delivered – nationally – within 48 hours.
