About Navigator Casuals

Navigator-Blurring the lines between casual and formal

The cosmopolitan look is in. Stuffy formal wear belongs in the boardroom. Instead, the youth are gunning for formal clothes that wear casual, yet have a trendy finish.

This is where Navigator steps in. This designer brand boasts a colourful selection in stylish formal wear. Aggressively bright tones to earthy ones that don't cost the earth; Navigator has established itself as a designer chain that keeps it real, yet consistently delivers a quality product. They do this by make their own garments. Starting from acquiring the yarn and dyeing it, a tight watch is kept on every stage till each individual piece is ready to wear.

The formal collection is marked by high quality fabric-the cotton and linen is imported from Italy-and the designs are stunning to say the least. This comfortable wear is complemented by elegant cuts that allow it to suit just about every occasion. If it's time for business, Navigator's 100% linen collection will never fail to impress. In trousers, the brand offers plenty of choices in corduroys and textured fabrics in flat fronts, pleated and boot cuts. For those who prize exclusivity, Navigator's in-house designers often come up with limited collections that reflect global trends like frayed edges, snazzy detail and snap buttons.

To catch these all one needs to do is walk into any one of the retail outlets, two in Chennai, one at Spencer's Plaza, Mount Road, and the other at Kasi Arcade, T.Nagar and also at Garuda Mall, Magarath Road, Bangalore. As the move to comfort wear catches on, the brand has plans to open up more outlets all over South India. So Navigator clientele have a promising fashion year to look forward to.
