About tajonline.com

Tajonline.comT was created in 1997 and is today the most popular e commerce portal catering to Non Resident Indians (NRIs) across the world. The vision was to be the premier fulfiller of all the needs of the non-resident India ("NRI") community residing in the United States and worldwide and the leading platform for delivery of products and services to this segment. The site allows non-resident Indians the ability to gift high quality merchandise to friends and family in India. Creation of this service enabled the company to put in place a large logistics system and access to high recognition brands in the Indian market. What makes the services of TajOnline.comT truly unique is the combination of personalized service, or tailor-made solutions, combined with surprisingly reasonable prices.

Tajonline currently has vendor relationships with prestigious vendors in India with arrangements to supply products and to deliver a drop-ship basis. We have come a long way from the humble beginning, where about 50 products were initially offered to our clients. Tajonline.comT currently boasts more than 3500 products covering reputed brands and quality products, which can be delivered across 1500 locations in India.

At Tajonline.comT, our number one priority is providing the best service possible to our customers - and that is how we retain our clients. They turn to us for every gift-giving occasion, knowing, from first-hand experience, that we can be relied upon. What distinguishes TajOnline.comT from any other player in the fast-paced market today is the quality of its service - careful packaging and shipping of the quality products on offer. Dedication to the client is clearly visible from the fact that we personally answer email and phone calls. With well experienced staff in our team, we are well equipped to service any client requirement. The resulting efficiency in service as well as the quality of products offered ensure customer retention.

As a result of the excellence that is prevalent at Tajonline.comT, our customer base has expanded to include not only in those clients who are based in the USA but also in other countries such as the UK, Canada, South Africa, Thailand, Malaysia, France, Germany, Australia, New Zealand, and the UAE, among others.

For keeping in touch with loved ones based in India, Tajonline.comT also offers calling card services for NRIs, or for Indian traveling to the US. The calling card services offered by TajOnline.comT strictly adhere to the guidelines of quality calling cards at the best rates thereby providing value for money to the client.

Tajonline plans to use the experience gained by catering to the gifting requirements of the typical NRI to expand the number of its properties and thereby the width of services required by the ethnic purchaser. The upcoming products include - shopping for local Indian customers, a wedding store that will cater to needs of wedding related purchases, consultancy, information for clients, among others. The launch of these new verticals will significantly enhance the scope of products as well as the customer base.

Tajonline.com's objective is to become the one stop solution provider for Indian nationals as well as NRIs, by providing a wide range of services and products. And we are well on our way to achieve this goal!
